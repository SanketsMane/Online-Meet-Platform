Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Media Encryption

Kidokool uses Secure Real-time Transport Protocol (SRTP) for end-to-end media encryption. Your video and audio streams are encrypted between your browser and our SFU server.

SFU Architecture

We utilize a Selective Forwarding Unit (SFU) topology. This means each participant sends media to the server, which then forwards it to others. This architecture is efficient and secure, allowing for high-capacity rooms without compromising your direct connection privacy.

Recordings

When you record a meeting, the data is stored temporarily in your browser's local storage (Blob). Once the recording is stopped, it is downloaded directly to your device. We do not store your recordings on our servers.

Analytics

We use aggregated usage statistics to improve our service. We have no intention of using or selling your personally identifiable data.
