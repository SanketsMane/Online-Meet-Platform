Real-time Simple Secure Fast video calls, messaging and screen sharing.

Our Mission

tawktoo is a powerful, open-source WebRTC platform designed to provide seamless real-time communication directly in the browser. Built with performance and privacy in mind, tawktoo leverages advanced technologies like Mediasoup (SFU) to ensure high-quality audio and video experiences even in large-scale meetings.

Whether you're hosting a private session or a developer integrating communication into your own app, tawktoo provides the tools you need for modern digital collaboration.
