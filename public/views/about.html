Built on advanced WebRTC technologies and the Mediasoup Selective Forwarding Unit (SFU), tawktoo ensures that your meetings are not just fast and reliable, but also incredibly secure and private.

Technical Excellence

Our architecture is designed for the modern web. By leveraging SFU topology, we enable multi-participant video conferences with minimal latency and high efficiency. This means more stable connections, even on limited bandwidth, and a smoother experience for everyone involved.

Privacy by Design

Your privacy is not an afterthought; it's our core principle. We do not store your media streams on our servers. Recordings are handled locally in your browser, ensuring that sensitive conversations remain between you and your participants.
